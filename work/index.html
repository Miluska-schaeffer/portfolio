Web Development

A selection of some of the projects developed during Le Wagon Bootcamp Oct-Dec 2020

These were developed on Ruby On Rails and deployed on Heroku and are
displayed here chronologically, being the last one the final project of the bootcamp.

Mixology, a prototype of an application to find and share drinks recipes.

Developed in Rails 6 and deployed to Heroku. Includes the use of SCSS, Bootstrap.

View Project

AddressMover Trucks, an Airbnb clone, is a platform where users can find and offer trucks for rent.

Developed in Rails 6 and deployed to Heroku, includes SCSS, Bootstrap, Devise, Pundit, Mapbox, Geocoder, PgSearch, EasyAutocomplete.

View Project

AddressMover, an application that allows users changing their address information from their service providers easily.

Developed in Rails 6 and deployed to Heroku, includes SCSS, Bootstrap, Devise, Pundit, Mapbox, Geocoder, PgSearch, EasyAutocomplete, Wicked PDF, Sucker Punch, AJAX, ActionController::API, Stimulus.

This app prototype required the creation of a fictitious service partner called "Travel Dream", to showcase the API - the address change - in action.
